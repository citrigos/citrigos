🐾 **Animal Fact:**  Many Chinese philosophers believe that the universe is made from two opposing forces, the Yin and Yang. The panda is one symbol of this philosophy with its contrasting black-and-white fur. The Chinese believe that the gentle nature of the panda demonstrates how the Yin and Yang bring peace and harmony when they are balanced.  <br/> <br/>*Last Updated:  Sun Apr 19 00:16 PT*
