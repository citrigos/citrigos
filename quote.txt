🐾 **Animal Fact:**  Someone who suffers from "anatidaephobia" believes that somewhere, somehow a duck or goose is constantly watching them. The word "anatidaephobia" is from Greek word anatidae, which means ducks, geese or other water fowls, and phobos is Greek for fear.  <br/> <br/>*Last Updated:  Sat Feb 28 13:19 PT*
